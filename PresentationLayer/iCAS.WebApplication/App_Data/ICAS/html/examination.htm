ACADEMIC_Examination

  1. Normally two examinations are conducted by the college in one academic session beside, the CHSE/University examinations.
  2. For +2 IInd year, +3 Ist year, +3IInd year and +3 IIIrd year Pre-Test and Test-Examination will be conducted in the months of September to October and December respectively. However examination committee chaired by the Principal reserves the right change. The scheduled of the examination for the smooth conduct of the examination and need of the CHSE/University.
  3. For the +2 Ist year students Half-yearly and Promotion Test examination will be normally conducted during the month of December and April or any other date on deemed suitable to the college administration.
  4. The exact date, programe, duration, full marks and guidelines, if any of the examination will be notifies 15 days before the commencement of each examination.
  5. Willful absence from the examination shall make a student liable for time or detention in that class, However principal can grant exemption subject to the condition he/she will appear the re-examination at a later date by depositing the requisite fee for it.
  6. Malpractice and misconduct in the examination hall woll leads to exclusion/detection/fine, as deemed fit.
  7. In order to get promotion to the next higher class or to be declared setup in the Test Examination, a student should have 75% of class attendance and should clear all dues payable to the college in addition to satisfactory performance in the examination.
  8. The CHSE, Orissa conducts two examination every year for +2 students; such as, a)Annual Examination from the 1st week of March or as nitified by CHSE, ORissa and Instant Examination, for the unsuccessful candidates after the publication of the result. Unsuccessful candidates who have secured pass mark in aggregate are eligible to appear the Instant Examination in the failed subjects only, as per the programme notified by the CHSE, Orissa for the purpose.
