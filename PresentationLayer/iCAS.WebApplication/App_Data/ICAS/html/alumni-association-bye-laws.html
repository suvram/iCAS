BYE-LAWS OF - ALUMNI ASSOCIATION

TENTULIA SASAN DEBASTHAN COLLEGE

TENTULIA SASAN DEBASTHAN COLLEGE, BIJAYA DHANURJAYA PUR, GANJAM

NAME OF THE SOCIETY:

The Name of the Society shall be “Tentulia Sasan Debasthan College Alumni Association” and shall be referred to as the Association subsequently in these chapters.

ADDRESS:

Tentulia Sasan Debasthan College Alumni Association, T.S.D. College, Bijaya Dhanurjaya Pur, Ganjam, PIN – 761120.

OBJECTIVES:

i. To encourage the members of the association to take active interest in the activities and progress of the Alma mater.

ii. To provide assistance by all means in attaining better education to the needy students and general public.

iii. To act as agency for implementing all social service activities, development activities implemented by government and other organizations or through sensitization.

iv. To promote and encourage friendly relations among all the members of the Association in the process of discharge of duties.

v. To keep Alumni informed about the Alma mater.

vi. To promote and propagate the achievement of the members and the Alma mater through exhibitions, news letters, Media, Electronic Mail, etc.

vii. To promote academic and professional excellence by organizing or sponsoring Seminars/Workshops/Conferences/Guest Lecturers/Publications, etc. for benefit of the members, students and society at large in the college as well as in the villages around.

viii. To organize Cultural, recreational, social, professional and development activities beneficial to the Alma mater, members and society at large.

ix. To provide career guidance in both academic and extra-curricular field beneficial to the public at large and students of Alma mater and to create opportunities for transfer of technology/knowledge among the members and the Alma mater.

x. To facilitate employment/campus placement for the students, members and public at large within legal frame work.

xi. To accept any gift, donation, grant or subscriptions towards or to accumulate and provide a fund and invest the same and apply the income arising there from or to resort to the capital thereof for any of the object of the alumni association.

xii. To invest any money at the disposal of the alumni association in such manner as may be determined from time to time.

xiii. To promote social commitment for the benefit of the Indian Society.

xiv. To serve the common cause of the members’ in general.

xv. To promote social and communal harmony and social mobility.

xvi. To promote and sensitize better and efficient methods of agriculture and other occupations.

RULES AND REGULATIONS:

LIFE MEMBERSHIP:

All Alumni of the college (T S D College) shall be eligible for Life Membership of the Association subject to they register themselves as members following due procedure given hereafter.

ASSOCIATE MEMBERSHIP:

All regular faculty members (present/retired/transferred/resigned) shall be Associate Members. They may take part in all activities of the association. They shall have participatory rights in discussions, be members of the sub-committees, can act as advisors and can be co-opted as members of various committees of the association but shall have no voting rights.

HONORARY MEMBER:

A person of eminence (Senior executive of Govt./PSU/MNC/a big Indian Company or a person of International/National/State repute can be selected as Honorary Member and for this prior approval of the executive committee shall be obtained.

MEMBERSHIP FEE: (For all type of members except Honorary members)

Yearly membership fee: Rs. 100/-

Life membership fee: Rs. 400/-

CEASATION OF MEMBERSHIP:

A member of the association shall cease to be such member if he/she resigns or found to be of unsound mind or on death or is convicted by a court of law or termination of the membership by the Executive Committee for compelling reasons and approved by the General Body.

GENERAL BODY:

Ø There shall be a general body of the association consisting of all members.

Ø The General Body shall consist of all members of the Association.

Ø The General Body shall hold the Annual General Body meeting at least once a year and not more than 18 months shall elapse between two successive Annual General Meetings.

Ø A prior notice of 21 days for convening an Annual General Meeting shall ordinarily be given to the members by the Secretary in consultation with the President.

Ø The notice should be given by letters or through newspaper or by web notification or e-mail or SMS.

Ø An emergency meeting of the General Body can be convened by the President in compelling situation by short notice.

Ø The quorum for the General Meeting shall be 1/10th of the total members registered their names.

DUTIES OF GENERAL BODY:

Ø To set guidelines for the executive committee so as to achieve the objectives of the Association.

Ø To consider and adopt the Annual Report and (Audited) Annual accounts of the association.

Ø To approve the budget of the following year.

Ø To honour the Jubilee Alumni and distinguished alumni.

Ø To elect the office bearers and members of the executive committee.

Ø To transact any other business with the permission of the President.

EXECUTIVE BODY:

Ø There shall be an Executive Committee to manage affairs of the Association and the tenure of the EC shall be for two years.

Ø The Executive Committee shall consist of

i. President - 1

ii. Working President – 1

iii. Vice President – 2

iv. Secretary – 1

v. Joint Secretary – 2

vi. Treasurer – 1

vii. Member – 7

Out of the total executive committee members minimum five members should belong to woman category.

ELECTION OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE:

Ø The Principal, T.S.D. College (TSDC) shall be the ex-officio President of the Executive Committee.

Ø All other office bearers and members of the executive committee shall be elected.

Ø The immediate past Secretary shall be a member of the EC.

Ø The procedure for election of the office bearers and members shall be regulated according to the bye-law of the association.

Ø Any member of the Association can contest for the election of EC.

Ø One of the Vice-Presidents and Joint Secretaries shall be nominated by the President amongst the Associate Members who will coordinate the Alumni and the Alma Mater.

Ø No elected/nominated office bearers/members of the EC shall hold office for more than two consecutive terms.

Ø In case of any absence of members/office bearers of the EC, the General Body shall nominate person/persons to fill in the vacancy/vacancies from the members present in the General Body Meeting.

Ø In case of dissolution or combined resignation, the Principal is vested with the powers to reconstitute the EC within six months of dissolution by convening a urgent General Body Meeting.

BUSINESS OF EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE:

Ø The Executive Committee shall ordinarily meet at least four times a year.

Ø A prior notice of 7 days shall ordinarily be given to members for convening a meeting by the Secretary in consultancy with the President.

Ø The quorum for the Executive Committee Meeting shall be six.

Ø In case any vacancy arises in the Executive Committee, the Executive Committee shall fill in this vacancy by nomination. A vacancy would be deemed to arise when a member/office bearer of the EC expresses his/her inability to discharge responsibility or when a member remains absence for three consecutive EC meetings without prior information. It should be approved by the General Body.

Ø The Executive Committee shall have all powers with regards to management and promotion of the objectives of the Association according to the directives of the General Body.

Ø The administration of all the affairs of the Association vests exclusively in the EC and the same shall be conducted, executed, administered and managed through the members of the EC.

Ø The EC may formulate and enforce such rules and regulations as are required for proper running and administration of the alumni association, which are binding on the members of the alumni association.

Ø The Executive Committee shall frame/amend bye-laws in accordance with the rules and regulations and report to the General Body. The said bye-laws will be approved by the General Body in its meeting before enforcement.

POWERS AND DUTIES OF THE OFFICE BEARERS:

PRESIDENT:

Ø The President shall preside over the Executive Committee Meetings and the General Body Meetings and shall have control and regulation of all the affairs of the association.

WORKING PRESIDENT:

Ø The Working President shall act for the President in his absence. He shall have all the powers for the management and promotion of the objectives of the Association.

Ø The Working President shall have powers to convene the meeting of EC in case of absence of the Secretary.

VICE PRESIDENT:

Ø The Vice-President shall assume in place of the President and Working President and perform his duties whenever both the Presidents are absent.

Ø The Vice-President shall also perform such other duties as specified by the EC from time to time.

SECRETARY:

Ø The Secretary shall arrange, co-ordinate to conduct all the meetings of the alumni association.

Ø He shall maintain all the minutes of the meetings of the alumni association.

Ø He shall assist in the administration and management of the alumni association to the President and the EC and perform all incidental duties which are necessary in administration of the alumni association.

Ø He shall maintain and custodian of all the records/files of the association except the account records.

JOINT SECRETARY:

Ø The Joint Secretary shall assist the Secretary and shall perform all such duties as may be delegated and assigned to him by the Secretary or EC.

Ø The Joint Secretary will act as Secretary in case of latter’s absence.

TREASURER:

Ø He shall be the custodian of all the funds and valuable securities of the alumni association and shall be responsible for keeping full and accurate accounts of all receipts and disbursements in the appropriate books maintained by the alumni association.

Ø He shall be responsible for collection of all the dues to the alumni association.

Ø He shall submit periodical/annual statements of accounts to the EC.

FINANCE AND ACCOUNTS:

Ø The association shall raise funds for pursuing the objectives of the Association through:

i. Money donated by the members

ii. Subscriptions of the members

iii. Any other sources approved by the EC

Ø The accounts of the association shall be maintained in any nationalized bank and can be operated jointly by the Treasurer, Secretary and President.

Ø Financial year of the association shall be from 1st April to 31st March.

Ø The balance funds of the association may be invested in Bonds/Fixed deposits by prior approval of the EC.

Ø The money raised from membership shall not be spent for any purpose. However, the interest raised from various investments can be spent to fulfill the objectives of the association.

AUDIT AND ACCOUNTS:

Ø The accounts of the association shall be audited once in a year by a Chartered Accountant to be appointed by the EC after these are duly audited by an Internal Auditor to be appointed by the EC.

RECORDS OF THE ASSOCIATION:

Ø The following records shall be maintained in the office of the association.

i. Roll of Members with full address

ii. Minutes of the EC and General Body

iii. Stock Registers

iv. Cash Book and Ledgers

v. Receipt Books

vi. Voucher files

vii. Copies of all publications and reports

Ø Every member of the association shall have the right to of inspection of records of the Association during the office hours.

Ø The disposable records, if any, may be disposed off/destroyed with prior permission of the EC.

SUIT AND PROCEEDING BY AND AGAINST THE ASSOCIATION:

Ø The Secretary or any other office bearer authorized by the EC may sue on behalf of the association. When the association is being sued, the Secretary will represent the Association.

AMENDMENT OF RULES AND REGULATIOS:

Ø Amendment to the rules and regulations shall be carried out only by a General Body Meeting when minimum three-fifth of the majority votes of the members present in the meeting agrees upon the amendment. The proposed amendment to the rules and regulations shall be circulated to the members of the General Body as an agenda of the meeting.

WINDING OF THE SOCIETY:

Ø The winding up of the association shall be carried out as per relevant clauses of Societies Registration Act.
