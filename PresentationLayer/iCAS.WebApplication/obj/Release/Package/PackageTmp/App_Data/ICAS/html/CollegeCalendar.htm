ADMISSION

1. Admission in to different classes of the college can not be claimed as a matter of right. The Principal reserves the right to refuse on cancel any admission with out assigning any reason there of.

2. Merit and conduct are the most important criteria for admission.

3. After the participation of the results of HSC/CHSE examination application for admission is to be made in the prescribed form which can be obtained from the college counter on payment of Rs.50/- (fifty) for +2 and +3. Prescribed admission period is given below.

1) +2: The normal admission into +2 wing with out fine in six weeks from the date of publication of HSC result or re-opening of the college which one in later; similarly the normal period of admission with a time of Rs.200/- is two weeks there after.

2) +3: The admission period into +3 wing with out fine is two weeks with the date of the publication of CHSE result or re-opening of the college, which one is later, similarly the period of admission with time of Rs.100/- is 10 days there after.

4. Application for admission received after the expiry of the last date (15 days from the date of publication of the qualifying examination or as notified by the college authority shall not be entertained.

5. Selected candidate shall be intimated by post in the prescribed information letter regarding the date and time of admission, allotted subjects and fees payable to the college.

6. Admission of the application will be taken up for follow up step on production of valid information letter signed by the principal or by any other competent authority. College will not be held responsible for any kind of postal lapse.

7. Selection committee, constituted by the authority of the principal reserves the right to regulate the process of admission into different classes. Committee can frame any other rule in addition to the existing rules to be implemented for admission with due approval of the principal.

8. Selection to the Honours classes shall be made after the admission process is over. However students have to specify their choices in order of preference in the application forms for admission into +3 Arts stream.

9. Students passing from Boards/University other than CHSE, Orissa/ Berhampur University have to produce the migration certificate at the time of admission, failing which his/her admission shall be cancelled.

10. In case of any doubt relating the status of the Boards/University applicant on demand have to produce equivalent certificate issued by the competent authority, failing which his/her selection for admission shall be cancelled.

PRODUCE OF ADMISSION

1. Application form for admission is to be produced from the college counter on payment of Rs.50/- or determined and notified by the college authority on their stipulated time.

2. The complete application form shall be enclosed with Xerox copies of the school leaving certificate, conduct certificate, mark sheet of the qualifying examination, three passport size photographs duly attested by a Gazetted officer of the Govt. of Odisha along with other testimonial as required by the college authority.

3. No application for admission shall be received after the last date.

4. The original certificates, conduct certificate and mark sheets ate to be produced at the time of admission for verification.

5. The Index and Intimation card attached to the application form must be properly stamped and self addressed.

6. Any change in the postal address must be communicated to the principal in writing.

7. Application for admission incomplete in any form shall be rejected.

8. Index card will be despatched soon after the receipt of the application form with a code number, which is to be quoted for subsequent correspondence regarding admission. However index card does not confreres the guarantee for getting a seat for admission. It merely confirms the receipt of the application for admission.

9. After scrutinizing the application admission committee will prepare the merit list and accordingly intimation card shall be sent by post only. For any postal lapses college authority will not be held responsible.

10. if in any case the candidate does not take admission on specified date as per intimation letter, his/her claim for admission will be forfeited.

11. From the date of admission he/she became a bonafied student of the college and she is to be guided by the college rules and regulations on framed from time to time.

12. After admission each student (+2 & +3) will be assigned with a roll number recorded in the admission register of the college and there after all correspondence relating his/her academic matters shall be made with that roll number.

13. As per the order of the Department of Higher education Govt. of Odisha, as framed from time reservation of seats for admission into +2/+3 1st year classes will be made to which no one challenge before the college authority.

14. Any applicant claiming a seat from the reservation quota must enclose Xerox copies of relevant certificate used by the competent authority duly attested by a Gazetted officer of the Govt. of Odisha along with the original application form and produce the original for verification as and when directed by the college authority.

15. Production of a certificate does not confer any right for a seat.
